## Getting Started Guide

{% details For Computer Science Students %}

### Week 1-2: Foundation Building

1. **Start with**: 3Blue1Brown Neural Networks series (visual intuition)
2. **Follow up**: Coursera Computational Neuroscience (mathematical foundation)
3. **Practice**: Basic perceptron implementation in PyTorch

### Week 3-4: Biological Context

1. **Read**: Selected chapters from Theoretical Neuroscience
2. **Watch**: Artem Kirsanov's neuroscience animations
3. **Experiment**: Brian2 simple neuron models

### Month 2: Choose Your Focus

- **Theory Track**: Deep dive into Neuronal Dynamics textbook
- **Applied Track**: Neuromatch Academy coursework
- **Research Track**: Begin reading recent NeuroAI papers

{% enddetails %}
